About CMI Bot

The CMI (Check Me In) Bot helps Discord communities manage member absences efficiently. Whether you're running a gaming clan, study group, or professional team, CMI Bot streamlines absence tracking with automated features.

  • Create and manage absence entries with flexible date/time options
  • Automatic role assignment and nickname prefixes for away members
  • Daily automated reports to keep leadership informed
  • Comprehensive CSV exports for record-keeping
  • Timezone support for global communities
  • Leadership tools for managing team absences
